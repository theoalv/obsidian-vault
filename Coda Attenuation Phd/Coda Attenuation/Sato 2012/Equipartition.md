**At late lapse time**
Specific intensity (I) is independent of azimuth (all direction) and horizontal position (uniform x and y)
--\> equipartition

Earth is layered media
Surface is free boundary (perfect reflector)
Bottom (moho) is transmitting boundary (energy leaks)
--\> even at late lapse time, there is vertical gradient
--\> energy leaks out below, intensity will decreases with depth
Horizontal isotropy is achieved at late lapse time
But cannot address vertical uniformity --\> incomplete equipartition

Because horizontal invariance is achieved, RTE equation is reduced to 1D form
