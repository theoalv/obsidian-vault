LHS --\> phase space flow, undisturbed particle move

RHS --\> collision integral
Loss term --\> relaxation time approximation, particles hit obstacle and scattered away
Less scatterer --\> longer relaxation time
Gain term --\> particle from other direction, hit obstacle, redirected to cell

Full equation
\[energy change\] + \[energy drifting away\] = \[energy bouncing in\] - \[energy bouncing out\] + source
