From 2D finite difference simulations of wave propagation in randomly inhomogeneous media
![image1](295a62d102df491f853f255080948347.png)
Observations:
- Scattered energy rapidly spread over the space behind the direct wavefront
- Coda envelopes at different distances converge to a common decay
- Coda amplitude is nearly uniform spatially behind S-wave at late time

All coda scattering energy is instantaneously and uniformly distributed behind the direct wavefront
- Scattered energy fills a sphere of radius V0t
- ![image2](a0e5bb4b14744855bc52e9a5a668b7c6.png)
- Outside the wavefront, energy is zero (H)
This model strictly separates direct wave energy and scattered coda energy
--\> different with diffusion that assumed all energy is mixed
