**Scattering characteristics**
Coda excitation model --\> coda is the result of multiple scattering by small scale heterogeneities
Incident plane wave have energy flux density of J<sup>0</sup>
--\> interacts with a scatterer --\> generates spherically outgoing wave J<sup>1</sup>
The amount of energy scattered per unit time for a solid angle dΩ = *J<sup>1</sup>r<sup>2</sup>dΩ* --\> surface area of a sphere
