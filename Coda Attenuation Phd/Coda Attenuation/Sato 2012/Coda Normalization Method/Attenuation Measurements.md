**S Waves**
S wave amplitude depends on:
- Source radiation
- Site amplification
- Geometric spreading
- Attenuation
--\> attenuation cannot be isolated directly from raw amplitudes

Aki 1980
S-coda carries the same source and site information as direct S-waves, but does not depend on source–receiver distance.
Idea --\> comparing direct S and S coda at same station and frequency --\> isolate attenuation

**P Waves**
Processing P waves are harder than S waves
- P coda is not dominant
- Late coda mostly S waves energy
--\> use S coda as reference for P attenuation

Yoshimoto et al. 1993
Assumed Wp/Ws is independent of magnitude within a small magnitude range (constant)
Even though P and S spectra are different
