Recorded seismogram --\> 3 inseparable effects
Amplitude = source x path x site
Isolate 1 or 2 effects to study an effect

Coda has characteristics **at late lapse time**
1.  S-coda envelopes have common shape
2.  Coda amplitude controlled by site amplification
3.  Path effects are nearly same for all station-event pairs
At sufficiently late lapse times, coda energy is uniformly distributed around the source

Idea --\> divide one observable by another effect --\> unwanted factors cancel out
S-code is used because is insensitive to focmec and stable across stations

Key idea --\> ratio of direct S-wave and S-coda amplitude

**Practical application**
Site amp CN --\> compare stations for one event
Source rad CN --\> compare events at one station
Attenuation CN --\> compare direct and coda from same waveform
